Game Features
Toggle Levers

There are many toggle levers throughout the world.
You will need to decide if you need to use one.

  • The Lever can jam. So try finding some lubricant.
  • The Lever Need to be powered to work.
  • There can be a time limit.
  • Not all lever will help you, some are a trap.
    So be ready to think fast.
Toggle the wrong lever and you might regret it
Pressable Button

There are many Pressable Button throughout the world.
You will need to decide if you need to use one.

  • There will need to be power to work.
  • There can be a time limit.
  • Press the Button to many time and you will Brake It.
  • But Watch out for damaged buttons.
    As they will kill you on the spot if you press them.
Do Not Push!
Pushable Boxes

You will find boxes that can be push to help you with the challage at hand

  • Boxes are color coded. Green to Red.
  • The heavy the box the harder it is to push.
  • If the box is light enough you can stack the boxes.
  • You can take the boxes from one area to a another area
    but if your caught on camera you will be punished.
Please do not steal the box
Batteries and Power Outlets

You can not escape the simulation without power
so you might need to find some power.
Power can be from an outlet or a battery

  • Battery have a power limit
  • Using the battery will drain its power.r.
  • Sometimes battery needs to be repaired.
  • You can recharge a battery with an outlet.

Please do not touch the + and - at the same time
Story
World Map

You are a leading researcher for a company called Researchacity and have been with the company for 5 years.
You wake up in your apartment because the phone starts to ring.
It the manager and he tell you that a new planet has been discovered,
a super-earth named "Kepler-452b" and they what you to travel there and see begin researching the planet.
you are told that with the new hyper engine we can travel there in just 3 months.

When you arrive on Kepler-452b.
you are taken to an island where Researchacity has set up a military compound and have an office set up in mountains.
You will go see the director who tells you.
You will be the first person to be visiting the mainland.
That is just 50 miles offshore from the small island Researchacity has selected for its base of operation and that you will be there for a few months.
you have been given some supplies A Tempery Base, a few turrets, and a lot of cemetery equipment.
you will also have 4 guards to help keep you safe.
You board a helicopter to take you to the mainland can take off for the mainland but before you arrive at the safe spot selected, you are hit by a sudden thunderstorm.

You are thrown out of the chopper by one of the guards with a parachute when you land you can no longer see the chopper.
All you have is the scanner you had in your pocket.
you will need to use the scanner to find some way to contact Researchacity and report the problem.
But on your first night, you notice that the wild animals are suddenly becoming aggressive and will attack on sight.
Build a shelter, Craft some weapons, Do your research and you might find a way to survive. But watch out there more out there than a few wild animals.
