VR Puzzle

Find Clues, Solve Puzzle, Find ways to beat the simulation

Game Features
Toggle Levers

There are many toggle levers throughout the world.
You will need to decide if you need to use one.

  • The Lever can jam. So try finding some lubricant.
  • The Lever Need to be powered to work.
  • There can be a time limit.
  • Not all lever will help you, some are a trap.
    So be ready to think fast.
Toggle the wrong lever and you might regret it
Pressable Button

There are many Pressable Button throughout the world.
You will need to decide if you need to use one.

  • There will need to be power to work.
  • There can be a time limit.
  • Press the Button to many time and you will Brake It.
  • But Watch out for damaged buttons.
    As they will kill you on the spot if you press them.
Do Not Push!
Pushable Boxes

You will find boxes that can be push to help you with the challage at hand

  • Boxes are color coded. Green to Red.
  • The heavy the box the harder it is to push.
  • If the box is light enough you can stack the boxes.
  • You can take the boxes from one area to a another area
    but if your caught on camera you will be punished.
Please do not steal the box
Batteries and Power Outlets

You can not escape the simulation without power
so you might need to find some power.
Power can be from an outlet or a battery

  • Battery have a power limit
  • Using the battery will drain its power.r.
  • Sometimes battery needs to be repaired.
  • You can recharge a battery with an outlet.
Please do not touch the + and - at the same time
Story
World Map

You wake up in a white room with just a medical bed in the middle of the room.
You get up and have a look around there is 2 speakers on the wall and a double sliding metal door in front of you.
Then a voice on the speaker says

"Welcome to the Simulation Vol-Vol-Vol-Vol Error 0x24212412. Volunteer, you have been selected to take part in the next phases of human trials. Please Stand by. ".

Then the door will open and you enter a room with 2 large boxes.
The AI on the speaker says

"Welcome to Trial 1. The boxes in front of you each weigh 500KG and you will need to use your increased strength to move them into the hole on the floor.".

You will then push the boxes into the hole without any difficulty And the door will open.
The AI says "New record. Volunteer 25412 Completed Trial 1 in the slowest time just. "

As you go from trial to trial you notice that the AI is going to kill you at the end of the trials.
Tou will have to find a way to escape via one of the trials before it to late
