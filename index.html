Game of Stick or Twist

You vs the dealer in a game of Stick or Twist
the dealer deals 2 cards to both you and him self.
The Objective is be closer to 21 then the dealer.
A Game of Stick or Twist can you beat the dealer!



Rules
  • The Ace card can be a 11 or 1, it your choice.
  • you have to keep the card you are dealt.
  • you can stick. then the dealer will take his turn.
  • you can twist. the dealer will draw a another card for your hand.
  • if you have 5 card and the total is less than 21 YOU WIN!
Created Using Python 3
