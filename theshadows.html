Harvestable Resources

You will need to look around the map for Harvestable Resources.

  • You will need wood for your home.
  • You will need metal for your weapons.
  • You will need oil for your electric generator.

Not all resources are available to you at the start of the game.
Research is required to unlock the ability to use
the Resources you find in the world.

Get you microscope out and do some research
Weapons

You will need there's if you get attack by any of the animals through the world.
There are a few different Weapons in the game.

  • Hatchet & Pickaxe
  • A Crossbow
  • A Pistol
  • A Machine Gun

But remember
You can`t just craft a Pistol, You will need to research a workbench and then find a blueprint for the pistol.
then you need the Resources to craft it.

Try fighting a saber tooth tiger without there's
Time of Day

A fully interactive day and night cycle.

During the day
You will go out and find all the resources you need and research anything you find because most of the dangerous animal are a sleep but not all so be Careful

But During the night
You will need to hide in your home or find a safe place to hide because the dangerous animal are know a wake and are looking for food.

Watch out for night
